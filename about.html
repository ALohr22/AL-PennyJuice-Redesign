About Penny Juice



Penny Juice is a company that focuses on tasty drinks, healthy products and of course above all - fun! If you’re looking for a sweet, healthy, and refreshing drink for a kiddo or even yourself, Penny Juice is the best option! Our variety of fun flavors and their quality ingredients are sure to impress and delight anyone who takes a sip. Afterall, choosing a Penny Juice product just makes cents! Based in Davenport Iowa, we are proud to say that all of our products are made in the continental United States and our ingredients sourced from local, and all natural farms.
